ABOUT US

Welcome to PONNAKARANTE CHAYAKADA, a restaurant located in AL Karama, UAE,
that specializes in traditional Ponnani dishes, shawarma, and snacks.
Our restaurant was established in 2020, starting with our first venture known as Marina restaurant in Sharjah UAE.
At PONNAKARANTE CHAYAKADA, we believe in providing our lovely customers with the best possible dining experience.
We have carefully crafted our menu to offer a variety of traditional Ponnani dishes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds.
Our chefs have years of experience and are experts in preparing the perfect blend of spices and flavors that are unique to Ponnani cuisine.
In addition to our traditional Ponnani dishes,
we also specialize in shawarma and snacks.
Our shawarma is made using only the finest ingredients and is prepared fresh daily.
We have a wide range of snacks available, including samosas, pakoras, and other mouth-watering treats
that are perfect for a quick bite on-the-go. At PONNAKARANTE CHAYAKADA,
we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service. Our staff is friendly, attentive, and always ready to assist you in any way possible.
We take pride in our restaurant's ambiance, which is warm, inviting, and perfect for a family-friendly dining experience.
Whether you are looking to enjoy a traditional Ponnani meal or craving some delicious shawarma or snacks,
PONNAKARANTE CHAYAKADA is the perfect destination.
We welcome you to come and experience the flavors of Ponnani cuisine at our restaurant.
